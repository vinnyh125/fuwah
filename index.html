Fu-Wah serves to deliver hearty, Americanized Chinese food that's grounded in the belief of authenticity, freshness, and commitment to the customer. Our expansive menu highlights the shifting and ever-evolving dynamic of cuisine and culture. At Fu-Wah, we are devoted to affordability and customizability, giving our customers near full-reign of how they would like their dish to be made.

While our dine-in service has been heavily impacted by the Covid-19 pandemic, customers are still welcome to sit-in and enjoy their meal indoors. However, due to the pandemic, all sit-in meals will be packaged and served in their takeout containers.
